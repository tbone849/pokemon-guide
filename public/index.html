What is this?

If you are like me, you are terrible with names but remember faces. This project was created with that idea in mind. There are two ways to go about finding the pokémon you seek. Clicking the 'browse' button will take you to a paginated listing of pokémon that includes a picture with their name. Pretty handy! However, if you know the name you are searching for, clicking 'search' might suit you better. On the search page, all of the pokémon names in the database are listed. You can enter a search term to filter down to your desired pokémon.

What kind of information can I find?

This guide is meant to be sort of a quick reference of basic information about each pokémon. When you click on any given pokémon, you will find basic stats, species information, and evolution chain (if any). Sure there are more comprehensive guides out there, but if you just need to find something quick, this should be your first stop. This project utilizes the Pokéapi and all data and images come from their database.
